Soal Determinan Matriks dan Adjoin

Soal Determinan Matriks dan Adjoin#

A. Hitunglah determinan matrik berikut dengan menggunakan rumus expansi baris

\[\sum_{k=1}^{n} (-1)^{i+k} a_{ik} M_{ik}\]

dengan \(M_{ij}\) adalah minior dari matrik A dan

\[M_{ij} = \det A_{ij}.\]

\(A_{ij}\) adalah submatrik dengan menghapus baris i dan kolom kolom j dari matrix \(A_{mxn}\) dengan \(1 \le i, j \le n\)

\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix}. \end{split}\]

B. Gunakan rumus matriks adjoin untuk menghitung invers dari matriks berikut dengan rumus

\[ (\operatorname{adj} A)_{ij} = (-1)^{i+j} M_{ji} \]

dan

\[ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det A} \operatorname{adj} A \]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix}. \end{split}\]

Jawaban A

\[\begin{split}A= \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Rumus:

\[det(A)=ad−bc\]
\[=(−7)(4)−(−5)(1)=−28+5=−23\]
\[det(A)=−23\]
\[\begin{split}A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Gunakan ekspansi baris pertama:

\[ \begin{align}\begin{aligned}\det(A) = 0 \cdot M_{11} - 2 \cdot M_{12} + (-3) \cdot M_{13}$$​\\Hitung minor:\\Minor $M_{12}$ :\\\begin{split}$$\begin{vmatrix}1 & −1 \\ 0 & 1\end{vmatrix} = (1)(1)−(−1)(0)=1$$​\end{split}\\Minor $M_{13}$ :\\\begin{split}$$\begin{vmatrix}1 & −2 \\ 0 & 0\end{vmatrix} =(1)(0)−(−2)(0)=0\end{split}\end{aligned}\end{align} \]

Substitusi:

\(det(A)=0−2(1)+(−3)(0)=−2\)

\(det(A)=−2\)

\[\begin{split}A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Gunakan ekspansi baris pertama:

\[det(A)=\sum_{k=1}^n (-1)^{i+k} a_{ik} M_{ik}\]
\[det(A) = 1 ⋅ M_{11} − (−3) ⋅ M_{12} + 1 ⋅ M_{13} − 1 ⋅ M_{14}\]

  1. Hitung Minor \(M_{11}\)

Hapus baris 1 kolom 1:

\[\begin{split}M_{11} = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} \end{split}\]

Ekspansi baris pertama:

\[\begin{split}=1 \begin{vmatrix} −3 & 1 \\ 1 & −3 \end{vmatrix} -1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 \\ \end{vmatrix} +1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 \\ \end{vmatrix}\end{split}\]

Hitung:

\(=1[(9−1)]−1[(−3−1)]+1[(1+3)]\)

\(=8+4+4=16\)

\(M_{11} = 16\)

  1. Hitung Minor \(M_{12}\)

Hapus baris 1 kolom 2:

\[\begin{split}M_{12} = \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix}\end{split}\]

Ekspansi baris pertama:

\[\begin{split}=(-3) \begin{vmatrix} −3 & 1 \\ 1 & −3 \end{vmatrix} -1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 \\ \end{vmatrix} +1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3 \\ 1 & 1 \\ \end{vmatrix}\end{split}\]
\[=(−3)(8)−(−4)+4=−24+4+4=−16\]
\[M_{12}=−16\]

  1. Hitung Minor \(M_{13}\)

Hapus baris 1 kolom 3:

\[\begin{split}M_{13} = \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix}\end{split}\]

Ekspansi:

\[\begin{split}=(-3) \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & −3 \end{vmatrix} -1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 \\ \end{vmatrix} +1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \\ \end{vmatrix}\end{split}\]
\[=(−3)(−4)−(−4)+0=12+4=16\]
\[M_{13}=16\]

  1. Hitung Minor \(M_{14}\)

Hapus baris 1 kolom 4:

\[\begin{split}M_{14} = \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix}\end{split}\]

Ekspansi:

\[\begin{split}=(-3) \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3 \\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} -1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3 \\ 1 & 1 \\ \end{vmatrix} +1 \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \\ \end{vmatrix}\end{split}\]
\[​=(−3)(4)−(4)+0=−12−4=−16\]
\[ M_{14}=−16 \]

  1. Substitusi ke determinan

\(det(A)=1(16)−(−3)(−16)+1(16)−1(−16)\)

\(=16−48+16+16=0\)

\(det(A)=0\)

Jawaban B

\[\begin{split}A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Determinan:

\(det(A)=(−7)(4)−(−5)(1)=−28+5=−23\)

Adjoin:

\[\begin{split}adj(A)\begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Invers:

\[\begin{split}A^{-1} = \frac{1}{-23} \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -\frac{4}{23} & -\frac{5}{23} \\ \frac{1}{23} & \frac{7}{23} \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Determinan:

\(det(A)=−2\)

Matriks kofaktor

Hitung minor + tanda:

\[\begin{split}C = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -1 & 0 \\ -2 & 0 & 0 \\ -8 & -3 & -2 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Adjoin (transpose kofaktor)

\[\begin{split}Adj(A) = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -2 & -8 \\ -1 & 0 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Invers

\[A^{-1} = \frac{1}{-2} Adj(A)\]
\[\begin{split}A^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 4 \\ \frac{1}{2} & 0 & \frac{3}{2} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Determinan

Dari hasil sebelumnya:

\[det(A)=0\]

Kesimpulan penting

Karena:

\[det(A)=0\]

maka:

\(A^{−1}\) tidak ada (tidak invertible)
